Synopsis

A heartfelt comedy-drama that celebrates the imperfect but enduring bonds of Filipino families, showing how love can heal even the deepest wounds.

Seven Sundays follows the Bonifacio siblings who are forced to reunite for their father's birthday celebration. What starts as a simple gathering becomes a journey of rediscovering family bonds, confronting old grudges, and learning to forgive. The film explores the complexities of Filipino family dynamics with both humor and heart.
